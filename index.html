Welcome to the Stanford MENA Alumni Association

Connecting Alumni, Deepening Relations, and Fostering Opportunities

About Us

The Stanford MENA Alumni Association serves as a bridge between Stanford University's alumni, students and community members from/interested in the MENA region.

Our Objectives

  • Build community in the US and abroad for our alumni and students via professional development opportunities.
  • Connect alumni with venture capitalists and startups.
  • Facilitate recruiting and distribution opportunities.
  • Organize events and activities that enrich our community.
